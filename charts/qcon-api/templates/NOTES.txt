Thank you for deploying {{ include "app.fullname" . }}.

1. Service
  $ kubectl get svc {{ include "app.fullname" . }}

2. Port-forward (if no Ingress)
  $ kubectl port-forward svc/{{ include "app.fullname" . }} 8080:{{ .Values.service.port }}
  Then open: http://127.0.0.1:8080/

3. Ingress (if enabled)
  Host: {{ .Values.ingress.host | default (printf "%s.%s" (include "app.fullname" .) .Values.defaultDomain) }}
